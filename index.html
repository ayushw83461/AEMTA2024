Scope of the
Conference

The conference aims to unite faculty, researchers, scientists, engineers, and scholars to exchange the latest advancements in advanced materials and their applications. Young researchers can showcase their work, and participants can network with experts. This initiative is organized by Sant Longowal Institute of Engineering and Technology, in collaboration with IIT Jammu, NIT Delhi, and IAHT Ludhiana as the National Conference on "Advanced and Emerging Materials for Technological Applications" (AEMTA-2024), conducted in a hybrid mode (online and offline).

All Information Related to the Conference

Mode of Presentation

AEMTA-2024 employs hybrid mode of presentation. A keynote lecture, invited talks, and contributed (oral and poster) presentations will be used to present papers in physical sessions (desktop sessions with all necessary facilities) as well as online using video conferencing and "Google-meet" platform.

Aim and Scope

The conference aims to unite faculty, researchers, scientists, engineers, and scholars to exchange the latest advancements in advanced materials and their applications. Young researchers can showcase their work, and participants can establish the possible research collaboration



Poster Award

During the highly anticipated poster session at the upcoming event, a distinguished panel of judges will meticulously evaluate and scrutinize the presentations. Only a handful of exceptional posters will ultimately be handpicked as the deserving recipients of the coveted Poster Awards, an accolade that celebrates excellence in research and presentation

Registration Fee Receipt

Registration fee receipt will be provided upon completion of registration for delegates attending the conference. This conference promises to be a great platform for scientists, researchers, and students from all over the country to network and exchange ideas.


Digital Certificate

Registrants for this conference will receive a digital certificate. This certificate will be useful in demonstrating your expertise and knowledge in advanced materials and radiation physics. This certificate will also help participants find work or academic positions in this field.
